
Ambassador of Egypt is to mobilize in investment.

It has been written by Katoma.

It has been published on May 9th, 2016.

The government has been advised to encourage many investors to come and invest in different sectors in the country so as to promote the country's economy at a fast speed.

Moreover, the government of Egypt has said that it has been doing awareness to the business people of that country to come invest in different sectors in the country including the health sector.
This has been explained by the ambassador of Egypt in the country, Yaredi, Elkana when he was speaking with this newspaper in his office in Dar es Salaam city yesterday about human donations that were given by the government to the victims of the earthquake in Kagera.
He said Tanzania's economy is growing by seven percent per year, but the government can increase the growth by opening the doors for more investors.
He added that Tanzania is doing well in the issue of promoting economy and that it can check what to attract more investors and grow the economy.
"My country has an intention to encourage investors from Egypt to come to invest in Tanzania for the benefit of both two sides," said Ambassador Eliona.
Speaking about the donations, he said his country has given donations to helping victims of the earthquake especially by considering the relationship that are between Tanzania and Egypt.
"It is a donation that was given out by Egypt in order to prevent them that were affected by the earthquake in Kagera province," he explained.
He said the government of Egypt has given drugs, other medical equipment together with tents of the value of 74 American dollars which is equivalent to 140 million shillings.
"Those equipment have already agreed to the office of the prime minister, the department of deaths because of being transported to Kagera," added that ambassador.
That donations has been made by the agency for cooperation and development of Egypt and the Hilary, from Egypt on September 10, this year, 17 people died and reported that 440 people were injured due to an earthquake of 57th 7 by using abusive language, "he said."
