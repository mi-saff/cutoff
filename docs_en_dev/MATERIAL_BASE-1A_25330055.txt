
TAMWA has congratulated JPM, Doctor Shein.

Has been written by our correspondent.

It has been published in 29th September 2016.

Tanzania Women's Association (TAMWA) thanking President John Magufuli and the president of Zanzibar, Doctor Ali Mohamed Shein, for the appointment of leaders and especially women.

In the appointment, several members of TAMWA got a chance.
TAMWA, I've heard its members and their positions are Mahfudhi Abdalla Omar, who has been the chairman of the board of directors of the Zanzibar Broadcasting Corporation (ZBC) and Raqqa, (secretary of the new district of Kigamboni).
TAMWA executive director, Edu Sanga said the institution believes that in bringing development changes in the country, the appointment of women in the high position is an opportunity that will enable the control of challenges in leadership so that there should be trusted and rights for all Tanzanians.
