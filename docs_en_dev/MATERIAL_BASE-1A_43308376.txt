It has been written by Pickup,.

It has been published yesterday, 13th December 2016.

Four children of one family and the residents of Bugesa village which is in the Lake Region, Sumbawanga Region, Rukwa Province, have been injured after falling on the wall of the houses that they had flooded.

The wall of the house occurred due to the heavy rains accompanied by strong winds that fell on the night before yesterday in addition, 23 houses have been destroyed by the heavy rainfall while in front of several families having no settlements.
Speaking to this newspaper through phone, the District Commissioner of Sumbawanga Region, Doctor Fred Haule said that the rains that began to rain for two hours starting at four o'clock at the night before the day before yesterday brought up a huge flared to the residents of that village.
While expounding, he said at the incident, the rains had been put up by 10 houses, while seven houses have collapsed and six houses have collapsed and six that have had reduced, where the number of livestock including chicken and cows was not immediately.
"All the four children of one family have been injured after falling on the wall of the houses that they had admitted to the health center for treatment and they have been allowed," said Doctor Haule.
He added that 22 families had more than one house in that village where now they have moved to their houses which were not allowed by the rains while they continue to renovate the rains.
"Even when he is the widow of his prestigious house which has been married by the rains, he too lives with the neighbors, also as the government we should give us a donation to the end of the maize and beans," he explained.
He said he arrived at the village to witness the destruction that was caused by the rains while the team of experts continues to evaluation so that to identify the losses caused by the rains.
Rukwa Regional Police Commander, George Kyando, has confirmed the occurrence of the disaster, but he said there is no one who had lost life in that event.
