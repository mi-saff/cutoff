The Zanzibar Football Association (TBN) in Arusha district has banned two players of the team, Mwokozi and their teachers Rashid Omar Hamza 'Larger' for one year to be involved in that game.

The players who have got that punishment is Issa Machibya, and Yahya Khamis Mkotya, who all of them are alleged to have defeated the referee while the coach Larger is expected to get into the field and start violence immediately after the end of that game.
The punishment that goes hand in hand with paying a fine of 100,000 shillings to November 6 this year, was issued by the executive committee of the party that was held at the end of last week after being satisfied with the evidence given by the referee together with the Singida Municipal of the game.
The secretary of the Simai Sheha team, Khamis Mkotya, has been faced with the punishment by involving themselves with sports issues for a period of one year as well as alleged to pay a fine of 100,000 shillings.
In addition, following that punishment also, the team's team had been charged a fine of 100,000 shillings which has been urged to be paid before their game being implemented on November 7th.
The team of Mwarija is claimed to have violence after the completion of the game between them and the Mlangali Village that ended in those teams from a draw of 1-1 goals.
