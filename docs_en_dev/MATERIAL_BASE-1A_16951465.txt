President RT will not vie for the leadership.

Has been written by our correspondent.

It has been released, October 25th, 2016.

Former president of the Athletics Tanzania (RT) Francis John has said that he does not plan to contest again for the leadership in the elections that will be held on November 27th in Dar es Salaam.

John led RT for six years starting from the year 2006 to 2012 and is the former head coach of the Athletics team that was participating in the 31st has been held in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, 2016.
Speaking on the phone yesterday, the coach who is the former coach, Felix Matundu has said that yesterday through the phone that he will not expect to contest for because he had already led in the very big position of RT.
I don't have a plan to vie for because I am not involved in the top position of national leadership here in the country, therefore, I will give him a chance since the presidency is the top position, "said Francis.
He wanted the president who will be selected in the election to give responsibilities to its members of the executive committee as he wants to work well and to promote the game in the country.
He said that most of the delegates of the executive committee of RT after being elected to involve yourself with activities of that game and to sit just as they see the president and they have not seen on the side.
