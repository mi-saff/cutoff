
The city of Calapan has a new dental Health Bus.

PO 1: 00, 2016.

Calapan, Oriental Mindoro, December 1 (Pia) - a new dental Health Bus was given by the Department of Health (DOH) Mimaropa in the local government of Calapan.

The objective of this is to give double the people who are needy particularly the residents living in far places.

A temporary dental health bus is a big help in implementing the program public service in Barangay where the government service will be sent to 62 barangays according to Scuous, which was appointed by the city.

According to Mayor Armada, Panaligan, the dental health bus will serve as nearest clinic, and it will be more than dental Team of the City Health which is being used by the patient's teeth when there is a scheduled visit to the barangay.
