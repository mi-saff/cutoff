
Every goal in Serengeti, 1 million.

Has been written by our correspondent.

It has been released on 20th September 2016.

The Minister of Information, Culture, Arts and Sports, Nape Nnauye has congratulated the national team of youth under the age of 17, Serengeti Boys in winning the home game to qualify for the African Cup against Congo witnessed, promising to buy every 1 million goal in the repeat game.

Previously, before that game, the minister gave 200,000 shillings to every player of Serengeti as a lesson and promised Serengeti if he will join Congo in the game, then every goal lies paying for 500,000 shillings, the previous day after the game.
In that game that was played at the National Stadium in Dar es Salaam the day before yesterday, Serengeti emerged with a win of 3-2 goals against Congo witnessed, they used 1 million shillings to three goals that they scored.
I thank Serengeti Boys in a right time to win by winning home in home, we have faith that they will do well, in the last games game, "he said and added that the government will continue to support them.
Still, that team has a journey to play in the last away game after two weeks, where if it will be successful by that team it will qualify to participate in that competition to be held in Madagascar.
Nnauye said that Serengeti has succeeded in its ability because the teams they meet is good and promised to pray for so as to do well in the next game.
