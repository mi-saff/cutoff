
12 to play with Uganda soccer.

It has been written by Grace Mkonga.

It has been reported, 30th November, 2016.

The coach of the Taifa soccer team, John Wanga has announced that the 12 players who will go in the camp to prepare for the friendly international game against Uganda that will be played on December 9 this year.

The Manager of the soccer of Ufukweni, Deo Lucas announced that squad yesterday on behalf of Benin who was not there due to an emergency.
He mentioned those players to be Ally Choki by, Rajab Mwillo, Teacher Kisongo, Mohamed Mpinga, Juma Ibrahim, Juma Duni Haji, Kassim Kilango, Mohamed Rajab and Samuel Sitta.
"That squad will come in the camp on December first this year to prepare for that friendly game as part of testing the strength," he said.
He said the goalkeeper Juma Kaseja was also among the problems, but later there is a possibility of an increase in others because of increasing strength.
