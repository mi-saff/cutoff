Poachers of Monduli, make sure that the classrooms do not sell the children.

It has been written by Bashir Nkoromo, Monduli.

It has been published today May 5, 2016.

The Prime Minister Kassim Majaliwa has urged the leadership of Monduli District Council to set aside money for the construction of classrooms and houses for teachers at the Lakya Primary School which is in the district to improve the level of success for the students of breeders.

In addition the community of breeders has been emphasized to stop the habit of buying female children, instead they get to the hospital so that they can be able to improve economically and the community get development.
Majaliwa gave the statement after visiting the founder of that school, Mujahideen, an old man who previously took the decision to build that school as a class one and to elect one of his children who had a primary school to teach them when he was going to school more than six kilometers.
Majaliwa said that the school has a lot of houses to be discussed by building other classes of classrooms so that the children and the neighbor of the elderly together with those involved in that school are able to study and succeed well.
He said that he has seen the shortage of classrooms together with houses for teachers and urged the leadership of Monduli District Council to set aside money in their internal revenue for the purpose of building houses for teachers and classrooms in that school.
He congratulated the artist for seeing the importance of education to its children, that I commend together with the neighboring children who are studying in that school to give advice to parents and guardians to stop the habit of selling female children, instead they go to school.
The head teacher of the school, Hasheem Mkwera, said that the school started in the year 2007 under the previous tree with 30 pupils, later getting sponsors where up to now, it has five classrooms and nine teachers with the same as 102 people as well as the death of the old man.
