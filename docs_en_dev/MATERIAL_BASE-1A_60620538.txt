
Quality Group, Simeone has invited tractors in the country.

It has been written by Hellen Mlenda.

It has been released, October 25th, 2016.

Quality Group Company Limited (QGL) in collaboration with Sinyati Enterprises Limited company has signed a contract of starting with machines and agricultural equipment in the country including tractors being part of supporting them.
Efforts by President Doctor John Magufuli of making Tanzania a country of industries.

Speaking after signing that contract in Dar es Salaam City, the Marketing Manager of QGL, Timochenko, said they have already completed the previous action of starting that factory which will be built in Morogoro province and its investment will be between shillings 50 and 100 billion shillings.
He said that at the moment the cost of buying tractors is high due to the cost of transportation until they go in the country, therefore, they start to bring in equipment and unite in the factory reduce costs and therefore farmers do modern and business farming.
The discussion that took place on 26th October, for more news.
