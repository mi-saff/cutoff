
A big ship takes place in Dar es Salaam city.

Has been written by our correspondent.

It has been published today, September 2016.

The Tanzania Ports Authority (TPA) has said that it is now open for a huge number to dock at the port of Dar es Salaam, because technology of the modern ship allows for.

Apart from the technology that was used to make that ship, the authority has improved their skills of its coaches after taking them to the South African countries, Denmark and France to get training on how to bring in the ship in the port.
The executive director of the authority, Deusdedit Kakoko said that after the coaches of the authority to succeed to bring in a big ship in the history of Dar es Salaam port with a length of 261 meters.
The ship called MV Mwanazuo's fees which has the ability to carry 4,3066 shillings has landed at the port of Dar es Salaam and to reduce 483 households and pit latrines before heading to Thailand.
Kakoko said the port of Dar es Salaam has the ability to serve the ship of that size in its four dock, which is a dock number eight, nine, 10 and 11 which have a length of 11 meters.
He said the coming of that ship is an important evidence that the port has the ability to receive a big ship of that kind, which were previously presented to the port of Mombasa.
Previously, TPA was seen to bring in another big ship with a length of 250 meters and later it brought to another ship with a length of 255 meters.
According to captain Mwikabe has said that the ship of 261 meters long time comes back to the port of Mombasa due to the shivered but the coming of that ship has been officially opened for business doors.
