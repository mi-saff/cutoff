
League board warned coaches.

It has been written by Raqqa, Pallidum.

It has been reported in 16th December, 2016.

All the clubs that are participating in the league sponsored by the Tanzania Football Federation (TFF) have been directed to employ coaches who are reputation according to the regulations.

The report of the chief executive officer of the league board, Boniface Wambura, went to a former head of the Premier League, the first level and the second is reminding them to employ coaches who have no reputation because it has been revealed that some people have no qualifications.
The report says the 72 regulations of all the league has aligned coaches who are supposed to lead the technical bench so as to go against the regulations is against the regulations of the professional area.
According to the regulations, the team's coach who is participating in the premier league is expected to have a license to start from bridge B of the African Football Federation (CAF) and the assistant coach is expected to have a bridge of C of CAF.
In the first premier league and the second league, the reputation of coaches from the country since the head coach is expected to have a bridge of C-CAF and the assistant is expected to have a certificate of position out of coaching.
According to those regulations, coaches who have no qualifications will be removed from the concerned team.
That regulations did a work at the end of the first round of the Premier League where the coach of Toto Africans, John Tegete was removed from the bench due to lack of reputation.
