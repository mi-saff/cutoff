Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA) in collaboration with the embassy of China here in the country have organized a seminar for the business community of China to educate taxes and investment.

That seminar will take place on November 25th this year in the city of Dar es Salaam and that it will help to provide education to those businessmen of China who are in the country to consider issues of tax.
While speaking in Dar es Salaam yesterday, the Director of Education in the tax of tax, Richard Kayombo, said the seminar will enable those businessmen to use tax laws easily and reduce taxes.
Furthermore, he said the topics which will be presented include changes in the budget of financial year, customs issues and control of goods, the tax tax especially in the construction sector and employment taxes.
Kayombo said that those businessmen will also get an opportunity to ask questions and get explanations on various issues so as to enable them to do their activities in accordance with the law and procedures of the country.
Chairman of the organizing committee from the community of those businessmen, Andrew Hodgson said Tanzania and China have had a long-term partnership in various issues.
He said the seminar that will be attended by more than 50 Chinese companies, will help its participants to learn different issues and especially in the tax laws in the country.
