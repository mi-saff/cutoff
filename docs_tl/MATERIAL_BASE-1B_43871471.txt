
Case of dengue, went down to Pangasinan.

August 19, 2016 April M.

Pangasinan, August 19) the number of people who got sick of dengue in the province of Pangasinan from January 1 to August 16 this year compared to the same time in 2015.

According to the record of the Provincial Health Office (LGU), around 1, 486 people got sick of dengue this 2016 compared to a higher record of 1,7590 last year.

Red Cross stated that seven were recorded dead due to dengue disease this 2016 while 12 were recorded last year.
