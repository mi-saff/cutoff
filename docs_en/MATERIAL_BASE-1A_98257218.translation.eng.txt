talks continue in Syria
August 24, 2014
the Syrian administration and the country's opposition side are expected to discuss the setting free of prisoners.
according to DW, a United Nations arbiter Lakhdar Brahimi said the meeting was a good start after an agreement was reached to send humanitarian aid to the conflict hit town of Homs.
moreover, the arbiter said the plan can be effected by sending an aid delegation to Homs between today and tomorrow.
hundreds of people in the ancient city live in deplorable conditions consistent with the occurrence of shelling day by day.
