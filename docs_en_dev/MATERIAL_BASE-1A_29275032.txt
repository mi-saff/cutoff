Nape to fight and are derailing the tickets.

It has been written by Grace Mkonga.

I was published in 1st October, 2016.

The Minister of Information, Culture, Arts and Sports, Nape Nnauye has removed fear from soccer fans on the availability of electronic tickets, while promising to fight and they destroy the program at an appropriate time.

Nape said that in Dar es Salaam yesterday when he spoke to the press about the tickets that were being presented officially today in the premier league match between Simba and Yanga.
Those tickets have started to be sold the day before yesterday but there was a challenge where some of the soccer stakeholders complained not being easily.
Nape said Selcom Company who are the organizers of those tickets to distribute cards to the National Stadium to ease the service starting yesterday morning.
Nape said that there have been a little challenge in the availability of those cards, but the issue has already been dealt with and that those cards are given free after a person bought tickets.
"This system is still a new system, there has been complaints that things are going on procedures, we are prepared to see this system successful because the challenges that came up will continue to be dealt with in time later things will go well," he said.
Nape said there are some people who have been selling in order to fulfill this system not to succeed in his work and promised they will face them.
He said that in any cost, he will ensure that the system does not know since the legal revenue will be used in government, in the team and all the people who are involved.
