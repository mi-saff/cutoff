
Two thousand the poor Bulos City benefited from the medical mission of US-based doctors.

January 28, 2016 Taduran.

City of Malolos, January 28 (Pia) -- there are two thousands of poor residents of Bulacan who benefited from the medical mission being conducted by the United States based on the medical mission in Bulacan Medical Center (VMMC), including the medical mission in Bulacan who gave the medical mission of the Department of Health owned by the medical mission of the local hospitals.
