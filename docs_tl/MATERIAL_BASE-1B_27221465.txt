
3 NPA rebels who were dead.
(Pilipino Star Ngayon), PO 2 44; Usahay, January 25, 2015 - 12: 00am.

Manila, Philippines - three members of the rebel New People's Army (NPA) were killed while many were wounded after an encounter with the military troop in the mountainous part of Barangay Mand-I, Surigao City the day before yesterday morning.
In the cellphone interview, Brigade General Jonathan Ponce Enrile, Commander of Arprice of 72nd Infantry Brigade, it was around 10: 00 in the morning when an encounter with the 45th Infantry Division (ID) is the group of NPA rebels in the area.
According to Ponce, the fight lasted for 15 minutes that resulted to the escape of their companions including a lot of those who were wounded.
Ponce said that there was a group of rebels encountered by the troops of the soldiers and the group of Pablo Lotion, Ka Lucas, who was responsible for the arson committed by a rich businessman at Barangay Mabini, city recently.
Two Milan rifles, a M203 grenade found out, a M653 rifle and ten Ford Everest, which contains personal equipment and grand documents. (Joy Cantos) were recovered at the scene of the fight.
