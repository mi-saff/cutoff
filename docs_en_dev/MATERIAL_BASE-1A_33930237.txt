
The general meeting of Simba on December 11.

Has been written by our correspondent.

It has been published today November 22, 2016.

The leadership of Simba Club has presented a meeting of an emergency members that will take place on December 11.

That meeting has been announced after the session of the executive committee of the club that happened the day before yesterday.
According to Simba's spokesperson, Haji Manara, that meeting will take place at the police hall hall in Dar es Salaam city.
He said the executive committee has given its efforts through article 22 of the constitution of Simba.
"The meeting will be a continuation of the normal meeting that took place on July 31.
2016, "said Manara.
However, that report has not explained the agenda of that meeting, but it is alleged.
That it may be used to improve the issue of Simba leadership and get into the issue of investment.
