
Small business people to relocate.

It has been written by Hanscana.

It has been published in 21st September 2016.

The Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar (UNBoA) has set aside Saateni area that is in Unguja town region so as to be used by small business people where all the important infrastructure is complete.

That was said by the minister of the country, the office of the president, regional administration and local governments, Haji Omar Kvitova when responding to a question by the representative of Mwesa constituency, Mihayo Juma Nunga who wanted to know why small business people are oppressed in doing their work in the Darajani areas.
Responding to the question, Kunzugu Ward said there is no violence of any kind being done by the government to business people until now, but the problem is for some of the traders to fail to follow the procedure and regulations.
He said that the government has built Saateni area for the purpose of small business people to do their work well and transferred to Mazengo Secondary School.
However, he said there are business people who benefit the order and continue to do those businesses in those areas where in the map of the city has been built for building a modern business buildings.
"The Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar has explained that there is no kind of violence done by small traders in Zanzibar, we have refused to go to Darajani, School, and we have set aside that area in Zurich city," he said.
He said the Darajani area for now is in the commercial purposes including the construction of modern shops.
