
From the parliament, President Magufuli moving to Dodoma officially in the year 2020.

Today, September 16, Prime Minister Kassim Majaliwa reading the speech to postpone the fourth meeting of the 11 parliament that has lasted for a period of two weeks, he insisted on the schedule of government moving to Dodoma.

"So as to achieve the government's intention to move to Dodoma, already it has been organized to enable the government to move to Dodoma for a phase, starting from September 2016 until the year 2020 without affecting our budget for the year 2016 / 2017.

1 - 2016 - February 2017.

The Prime Minister and all ministers, permanent secretaries and all executive secretaries who will move to Dodoma in the first phase. Moreover, every ministry will be required to relocate the servants of one department or two, and at the same time, they will be required to relocate the servants of one department to other departments in Dodoma.

2 - March 2017, August 2017.

This period will give opportunities to the directors of different ministries to put in their budgets of the year 2017 / 2018 costs of continuing to relocate its workers to come to Dodoma.

3 -. September 2017 - February 2018.

In this phase, the ministries will continue with relocating the servants of the departments that are inside the ministry.

4 - of March 2018, August 2018.

In this period, the ministries are supposed to continue to relocate its workers.

5 - of September 2018 - February 2020.

In this period, the ministries are supposed to continue to relocate its workers.

6 kilometers from March 2020 and June 2020.

During this period, the office of the president led by the honorable president of the United Republic of Tanzania and the Vice President of the United Republic of Tanzania will be relocated to Dodoma.

Prime Minister Kassim Majaliwa used this opportunity to urge all the ministry to start the electronic systems instead of moving from Dar es Salaam to come to Dodoma, we want Dodoma to be the electronic system, he emphasized the Prime Minister.
