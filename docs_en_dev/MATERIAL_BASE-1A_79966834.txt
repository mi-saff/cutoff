The Head of the police force in the country (IGP), Ernest Mangu has urged all police officers to ensure they are maintaining discipline, love, unity and peace in the country in order to protect citizens and their properties.

IGP Mangu said that after the exercise that was done between the police commissioner and the police of that region.
And the police commander of the special unit of Dar es Salaam, Simon Sirro has said the exercise is for the purpose of building the police force as well as identifying their responsibilities when they are not at work.
"Chairmanship will see the importance of this exercise where he decided to participate with us and urging all the police to ensure that they are maintaining discipline for all the time they are not in work," said Sirro.
He emphasized that the exercise will be sustainable so as to protect their health as exercises for those police is an issue that is required to be done every day in their lives.
