
Business people are expected to attend the conference.

November 17, 2016.

More than 50 companies are expected to participate in the business conference that is called Tanzania Comoros by Forum Forum which will be held on November 19 this year in the islands of Zanzibar.
In that conference which is the second in the country and a veteran politician in the country, Doctor Ali Mohammed Shein, there will be a debate about the increase in business, investment opportunities and cooperation between those two countries.
Speaking in Dar es Salaam yesterday, Anna Msonsa, who is the coordinator of that conference from the Tanzania Agricultural Development Party (TCCIA), said the conference will give an opportunity to the business people from both sides to know the opportunities available in these countries and how to cooperate in the issue of investment.
He said the conference will take place in Zanzibar, and it is also aiming at improving business cooperation from private companies from Tanzania and Comoros.
"It is good for all stakeholders and business people to arrive in large numbers to attend the conference because their presence will help to get an opportunity to discuss investment opportunities in all two countries, that is Tanzania and Comoros," said Anna.
He said that all the business people from those two countries who will arrive at the conference will get an opportunity to show their businesses to get markets easily.
The Tanzanian Embassy in Comoros, MKEWADAR, urged business people to arrive in large numbers to meet with their fellow traders of Comoros.
He said that the conference has had a great benefits since it was started, this is the second, the first one was held in Comoros by April 2015.
The conference is organized by the government of the United Republic of Tanzania through the Tanzanian Embassy in Russia, United Republic of Tanzania (TELC), TCCIA in the partnership with the business people of Zanzibar, industry and agriculture (ZTC).
