
ZFA using the national team coaches.

September 28, 2016.

Zanzibar Association of Zanzibar Football Association (CHAUMA), has said that it will make sure that patriotic teachers to make their national teams in Zanzibar.
That statement was given the day before yesterday by the party's president, Rwengenyeza, during a ceremony to hand over the license certificates to 26 coaches who had completed the training last month.
He said the national teams will be taught by people of Zanzibar themselves, who have the licenses of the African Football Federation (CAF) who were present.
"We have many teachers with qualifications to follow our team, therefore we cannot do not see the need to use teachers from outside to make sure our team," he said.
However, he urged the teachers to be ready to work for their licenses, which they received so as to reach those who were intended.
"Do not work well and to get a certificate is one, but the second is to release that profession that you have got and to the beneficiaries not intended," he said.
Furthermore, he said that they have a lot of teachers with licenses in Zanzibar and it is not little and others have already have licenses in that way to work as required.
He said in accordance with the regulations that they have made, any teachers who will not follow the regulations put in together with him.
