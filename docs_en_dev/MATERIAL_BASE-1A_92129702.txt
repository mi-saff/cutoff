
The vehicles that contribute to development in Arusha.

It has been written by John Mhenga, Arusha.

It has been witnessed, 15th November 2016.

Dharam Singh Hanspaul company which makes the board of vehicles of tourism in the city, has set aside millions of money for various development activities for residents of the city and its neighborhoods.

It is said that, it is one of the ways of supporting the efforts of the fifth term government of caring about the citizens of the poor.
While speaking at the launch of the bridge to connect Moshono locations, and Engutoto in Arusha town that was built with that company at a cost of more than 67 million shillings, the owner of the company, Hans Paul said his company is in hand in hand with the government in Arusha region for caring about the citizens of the poor.
Paul said the bridge was a savior by the donation to many citizens who had a great desire to cross especially during rains due to lack of reliable water, the elderly and motorcycle riders, are going on all the legs and there is no vehicle that had the ability to pass there.
He said that apart from building that bridge, the company has built three classrooms in EngenderWard for a cost of more than 50 million shillings, being one of the students going in class and to sit in the desks.
The owner said in addition to building and providing donations, also the company has employed more than 500 employees in three different companies that he had at their disposal, being one way of solving the employment problem in Arusha and here in the country as a whole.
