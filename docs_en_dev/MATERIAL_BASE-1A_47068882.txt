He opens a soccer station.

Has been written by our correspondent.

It has been reported, 24th November, 2016.

The center for improving soccer to youths who are under five to 18 years is expected to be opened on November 26th of Kitunda Ward in Ilala, Dar es Salaam.

Speaking to this newspaper, the owner of that center, former Simba and Yanga team, Ivo Mapunda said that he has seen the importance of teaching children with soccer.
"The station called Minister Mapunda Sports', the aim is to teach children the foundation of soccer from the small business so as to build a stable nation later," he said.
He also has apologized to all parents of Tandahimba in order to allow their children to learn soccer.
Procedures of joining the center are available on the launch of partnership with the local government.
He is among the players of Tanzania who have played soccer in great success.
