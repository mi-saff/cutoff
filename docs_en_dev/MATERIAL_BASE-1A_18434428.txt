
Al-SCE will not vie for presidency.

November 2, 2014.

Head of the army in Egypt, Abdul Razaq Badru, has said that in the explanation that was issued yesterday by Al-Shabaab's newspaper, he has decided to vie for the presidential election planned to take place on April this year, Mister Al-Solo, saying that the issue has been made and that it has no better way for the presidential candidate of Egypt.
