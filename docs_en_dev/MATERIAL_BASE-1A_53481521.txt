
The contractor of the bridge in Kinyagigi was given one day.

It has been written by Sophia Kaduma.

It has been reported, 24th November, 2016.

The Provincial Commissioner of Dar es Salaam, Paul Makonda, has given 25 days to the contractor of Yamoto Band, Francis Musiba, the construction of the bridge that deals with Tabata Primary School and Boncour.

Furthermore, he has directed the Municipal Council of Ilala, Mgalula, to ensure that he is partnering with the Tanzania Roads Agency (TANROADS) to inspect that construction as the quality and the required levels was arrived.
Makonda made that statement when he visited the Kinyerezi bridge and inspected the bridge as part of his tour in Ilala district.
Despite that order, he has also ordered the Roads Agency (TANROADS) and the supervisors of the project to inspect the bridge as it has a required levels before starting the use.
He gave that order after the citizens of that area continue to stop the completion of that project is started in the year 2013.
After the complaints, Makonda told the member of parliament of the Segerea region Bonna Kaluwa, if he once arrived at the bridge to inspect the construction where he admitted and joining the citizens to also accused the contractor for considering the construction of that bridge.
I have written here five times and the contractor who told me this bridge, is completed within four days since April 2016 but up to now it has not been completed and my citizens are continuing to get a lot, "said Boncour.
That answer was done in Makonda to put an open an agreement of that bridge was signed when he answered that the contract was signed on March 30th, 2015, and started on June 30th, 2015.
However, the representative of the Texas Company, Musiba, informed the construction reports to start the year 2013 and claimed to inza on 29th April this year.
This bridge is under the agency of roads (TANROADS), so we are waiting to get a permit to demolish some of the areas that are committed by this project from Ilala Municipality, "he said.
Makonda said that if the construction will not be finished in that period, he will not be given any contract in Dar es Salaam.
In another step, Makonda instructed the police commander of Kipolisi Ilala, Salum Hamida, to arrest the chairman of the Bonnele hotel, Salum Jaba, on allegations of not implementing its responsibilities including taking bribes, threatened to take 10 percent of the price that are sold.
