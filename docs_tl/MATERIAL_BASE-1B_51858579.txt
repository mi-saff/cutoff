
Military is on alert on the 25th anniversary of the NPA.

The Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP) is on alert after the celebration of the 25th anniversary of the New People's Army (NPA) today.
According to AFP Chief of Staff General Eduardo Año, the President Rodrigo Duterte still remains and has not yet taken back the order to chase the said rebel group.
It can be recalled that those who introduced themselves as members of the NPA in Quezon City as part of their anniversary which caused heavy traffic in the area.
Meanwhile, Año encouraged the leadership of the National Democratic Front (NDF) to control their armed men as proof that they are planning the peace talks in the government.
The fourth discussion of the government and NDF in April is scheduled to be rolled in April in The Netherlands.
