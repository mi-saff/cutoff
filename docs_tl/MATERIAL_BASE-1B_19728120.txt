Davao City - Mayor Sarah Duterte-Carpio is stopping the night market here after allegedly having a lot of violation on the ordinance by Davao City on the hundreds of vendors.
It is estimated that more than 700 vendors are affected by the mandate of Carpio regarding the start of Thursday along Roxas Avenue.
The step is based on the recommendation of the City Traffic and Transport Management Office under the chief, Rattle, Polilio Island.
