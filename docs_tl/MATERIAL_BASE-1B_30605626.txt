
AFP launched the offensive versus the BIFF.

Joy Cantos (Filipino Star Ngayon), PO 2 44- October 22, 2015 - 12: 00am.

Manila, Philippines - the military troop launched a offensive against the stubborn group of Bangsamoro Islamic Freedom Fighters (BIFF) in Brgy. Bulaga, Batan, North Cotabato yesterday.
According to Captain Joan Petinglay adds, spokesperson of the 6th Infantry Division of the Philippine Army, the purpose of their step to complete the forces of the BIFF is the cause of the forcibly evacuation of the residents of Pagalungan, Maguindanao and Batan, North Cotabato.
It is believed that many have died in the forces of the BIFF while they have not yet determined the time as the reason of the continuous fight between both sides.
Petinglay adds that they will not allow the damage of the BIFF in the said area and avoid the tension in nearby barangays.
It was said that the BIFF group was said to have been encountered by the killed 44 commandos of the Special Action Force (SAF) hid in seven barangays in the town of Pikit and nearby barangays after the bloody fight.
The law Enforcement Agency is to defeat the BIFF which is said to be holding ten guns of SAF commandos who were killed in the bloody encounter in Mamasapano, Maguindanao last January 25.
