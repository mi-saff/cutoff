Norway commemorates one year anniversary of seventy seven people who were killed by Anders Breivik in a bomb attack .
in commemorating that incident there have been memorial services in various areas of the country whereby in Oslo city people gathered at the government's headquarters because of that .
the Norwegian Prime Minister Jens Stoltenberg has explained that the attacker intended to change Norway something that he didn't succeed in .
he has explained that many people who were affected by that attack were young people .
reports say that attack has humiliated Norway which was proud of being a peaceful country and with great economic success in the world .
