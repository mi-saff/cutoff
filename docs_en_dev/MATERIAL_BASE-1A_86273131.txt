200 police officers who were seen to go against the police ethics including oppressing the citizens that are not guilty, they have been taken against them, they have been expelled from that step that has been taken in the period of January 2015 to June 2016 this year.

The deputy minister of internal affairs of the country Hamad Yusuf Masauni said that yesterday in parliament responding to a question by the nominated member of parliament Honorable Masauni, said the police force works in accordance with the law, regulations and procedures that are there.
However, there are a few police who have been doing ethics and good performance of our police force as outlined in the legal action that have been taken to servants who are seen to go against the ethics of the police work including beating citizens who are not guilty.
For instance, for instance, in the period between January 2015 to June 2016, 200 soldiers were taken against them and among them 152 were found guilty and sacked from work.
Masauni said that those steps will continue to be taken and insisted that all the police have been holding the law in implementing its responsibilities.
