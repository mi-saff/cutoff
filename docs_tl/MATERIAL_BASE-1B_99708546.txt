Dengue calamity in Tupi, South Cotabato By Betchai July 23, 2016.
The town of Tupi and South Cotabato finally put under state of calamity after jumping a four children who died and 300 others were placed under dengue.
Based on the data of Tupi Mayor Reynaldo Tamayo, three barangays in their town have high cases of dengue and this is the Poblacion, Acres and Mount ao.
In the said town, the killed victims were also from the age of 12 years old and below were crowding in their district hospital, which caused the state of calamity to declare a state of calamity to be used.
They also advised barangay officials and area Leaders' Summit to conduct cleaning in order to eradicate mosquitoes with dengue.
