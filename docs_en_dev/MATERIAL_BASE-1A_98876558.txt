
A large oil industry, sunflower farms are built in Dodoma.

It has been written by Swaliatu Msuya of Dodoma.

It has been published in 29th September 2016.

In Dodoma province, you will get a modern factory of grain production, so as to decide the sunflower and 300 people are expected to get employment in the factory.

The executive director of the National Social Security Fund (NSSF), Professor Godius Kahyarara has said that when he was giving a check of 397 billion shillings to the director of the Galijembe Girls Secondary School, John Maige, with the aim of buying food digestion system and that of production of cooking oil.
Those machines will be supported in one factory while speaking after handing over those funds, Professor Godius Kahyarara has said that the provision of those funds is in hand in line with the NSSF policy to invest more than its members in development projects.
He has said the policy of NSSF is to take industries that involve many citizens and even so, he said that the foundation stone at the factory will start working and to look at the internal and international market.
The Deputy Minister, Office of the Prime Minister, Labor, Youth and Employment, Antony Mavunde, said that the factory will give a lot of employment to the economy of the farmers, you will discover and grow.
Director of the Grain and Mazimbu Board, Maige has said the Grain and Mazimbu Board in collaboration with NSSF will implement that project.
The factory will have the ability to cater for 60 tons of maize by a day and to decide to produce 13 tons of fuel for a day, whereby the project is expected to produce one million employment opportunities and other employment opportunities including farmers.
Maige has said that the start of a natural gas factory in large numbers to decide the sunflower and the results of the implementation of the government policies to improve the economy of the country and the central economy of industries.
Director of legal services of Azania Bank, Geofrey Sabuni said that the bank has collaborated with the government to implement the policies of the economy of industries.
He said that bank is on the way to reach the farmers, the head of Dodoma Town District, Christine Mndeme has said through the start of that factory, many employment opportunities will be found and even the market will be found.
