in Uganda the government has begun a special campaign for cancer testing whereby more than one thousand women are expected to be tested in the first part of that campaign that will run for a period of two months starting next week .
the cancers that will be examined according to the authority here in the country are cancer of the cervix together and breast cancer in which statistics show that they have affected many women with its increase being mentioned to be growing at a speed despite the examination campaign a special device which has the ability to detect the initial stages of cancer is expected to be put in more than two thousand women .
